The Story

Born from the need to better organize filming locations. Born from the need to better organize filming locations. In 2004 David Rust, a CNN Camera Legend, showed me collection of notes he had made from his world travels. He detailed food, filming locations, hotels - the good the bad and the ugly and he wanted to share them. But at the time technology just wasn‚Äôt at a point to make it easy to take his collection of personal notes and put them in a place everyone could benefit.

Years later I had heard of another initiative to collect these details from the road warriors of news. But it too never got off the ground.

Then in 2025 I was shown a spreadsheet where crews could enter location information. But the spreadsheet had some issues: It was clunky, a user would manually have to search locations by clicking each city and state - how the details and locations were arranged. It also required three logins to use, completely impractical - a high barrier of entry. Also anyone could change details on a location OR DELETE IT.

This was my experience; I nearly deleted photos in a section for White House logistics. I knew we needed a better system.
